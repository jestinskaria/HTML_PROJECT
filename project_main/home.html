Welcome to Kidz Kare Toronto

Kidz Kare in Maple Leaf Square is a licensed childcare centre in the heart of downtown Toronto. We focus on helping children build strong academic and social foundations as they not only meet, but surpass all their developmental milestones. Our 6500 square foot facility creates a safe and comfortable learning environment for children from ages 18 months to 12 years
